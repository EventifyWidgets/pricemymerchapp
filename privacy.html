Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 05, 2025

Price My Merch ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information. All inputs—such as product costs, quantities, profit margins, or other calculator parameters—are processed locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information.

Local Storage

If you choose to save a calculation, the snapshot is stored locally on your device using Apple’s UserDefaults system. These saved items contain only the data you entered (e.g., cost values, quantities) and are never synced or transmitted off your device.

Sharing Your Data

You may choose to share calculation summaries via email or text message. When you do, the App opens your default mail or messaging client and pre-fills the message. The App does not send emails or messages itself, nor does it store recipient information.

Subscriptions

The App offers optional subscriptions via Apple’s in‑app purchase system. Payments and subscription management are handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive your payment information or any personal details from Apple.

Third‑Party Services

The App does not integrate with third‑party analytics, advertising networks, or backend servers. It does not send any data to external services.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store your personal data, there is no personal information for you to access, modify, or erase. You can clear your saved calculations at any time within the App.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please email us at support@pricemymerchapp.com.
