Price My Merch

Effortless pricing and quoting for your merchandise

All-in-One Calculators

Price My Merch gives you a suite of powerful calculators to price your merchandise, generate quotes, simulate drops and pre-orders, compute packaging costs, and determine break-even points. Each tool is designed to be intuitive and fast.

Retail Price Calculator

Quickly estimate retail price, profit margin and price tiers based on your production cost, labour and desired margin.

Bulk Order Quote Builder

Create customer-facing quotes with cost breakdowns, discounts, taxes and shipping – perfect for wholesale orders.

Product Bundle Pricing

Calculate bundle price, profit and margin. Support for common promotions like BOGO and multi-item bundles.

Pre-Order Profit Calculator

Simulate a pre-sale drop, including projected refunds and unit costs, to set the right pre-order price.

Packaging Cost Calculator

Calculate branded packaging costs per unit or derived from bulk quantities to ensure you cover every expense.

Break‑Even & Profit

Compute break-even units, revenue and margins by factoring platform fees and refunds.
