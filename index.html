About

Price My Merch is a tool designed to help creatives and business owners determine optimal pricing for their merchandise. Input cost, overhead, and desired profit margin to receive an accurate price suggestion for your products.

Features

  • Easy-to-use pricing calculator
  • Supports multiple product types and categories
  • Customization for shipping, taxes, and discounts
  • Helpful tips and resources
